Tutorial and Game Mechanics

Controls:

tap ← to move to the left by 1 pixel

hold ← to move rapidly to the left

tap → to move to the right by 1 pixel

hold → to move rapidly to the right

tap ↑ to move upwards by 1 pixel

hold ↑ to rapidly move upward

tap ↓ to move downwards by 1 pixel

hold ↓ to rapidly move upwards

Mechanics:

Use the controls to dodge the incoming meteors. Some meteors are faster and larger than others. Score goes up every time a meteor goes off the screen. The game ends when a meteor hits the spaceship. Hitboxes are sometimes larger than what you actually see.
